A Little About Myself

I grew up in Old Bridge, New Jersey and I have lived in the same area for the majority of my life. I played a lot of sports while growing up which I think has attributed to my competitive nature. I really enjoy trying to push myself and learn new things.

I graduated from Montclair State University with a Bachelors in Justice Studies. I initially had plans to become a police officer, but after some soul searching I realized that wasn't the right path for me. This realization ultimately led me to the Rutgers Coding Bootcamp for Fullstack Web Development.

After graduating from the Rutgers Coding Bootcamp, I became a Teaching Assistant and a tutor for the bootcamp. I'm constantly trying to improve my skills and I really enjoy trying to learn new languages.
